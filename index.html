Importance of Inspection

Property inspections are crucial for uncovering hidden defects and potential issues that could cost thousands in repairs. Whether buying or selling, a professional building and pest inspection provides peace of mind and protects your investment.

Our comprehensive inspections identify structural problems, pest damage, moisture issues, electrical and plumbing concerns, and safety hazards before they become expensive emergencies.

How It Works

  1. Book your inspection online or by phone
  2. Our QBCC-accredited inspector arrives on-site
  3. Comprehensive property examination conducted
  4. Detailed report delivered within 24 hours

What Is Inspected

  • Structural integrity and foundations
  • Roof, gutters, and drainage systems
  • Plumbing and electrical systems
  • Pest activity and termite damage
  • Moisture detection and ventilation
  • Interior and exterior finishes
Important: Always arrange your pre-purchase inspection before signing any contracts or agreements. This gives you the opportunity to negotiate repairs or reconsider the purchase if significant issues are discovered.

While not mandatory, a pre-sale inspection is highly recommended. It allows you to identify and address any issues before listing, potentially increasing your property's value and appeal. It also demonstrates transparency to buyers and can speed up the sales process.

Our detailed report will clearly document any defects found, including photos and descriptions. We categorize issues by severity and provide recommendations. You can use this information to negotiate with the seller, request repairs, or make an informed decision about proceeding with the purchase.
